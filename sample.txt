Maharana Pratap, also known as Rana Pratap Singh, was a renowned Rajput warrior and the ruler of Mewar in the 16th century. 
While historical records do not provide specific details about his physique, it is generally believed that he was of average height and had a 
strong and robust build. Being a skilled horse rider and warrior, Maharana Pratap possessed excellent physical strength and stamina, 
which aided him in his battles against the Mughal forces. His physical prowess and valor are celebrated in various historical accounts
 and legends, making him an iconic figure in Indian history.
